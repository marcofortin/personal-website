My Daughter

I look forward to the day
I can finally meet you.
When I can take you in my arms
and tell you how much I love you.

I have so many secrets to share with you
and so much knowledge to give you.

I already feel this connection between us
which submerges my life with brightness.
A beautiful smile in the morning
Joined by an endless good night kiss.

So fragile and innocent
I want everything for you to be perfect.

I am somehow afraid
Afraid to expose you.
We live in an imperfect world
And I sense the urge to protect you.

This world we live in
It is not one in which I want you to live.

Delicate as a flower
I want every other man to see you this way,
I want you to be treated with respect
And for you to respect yourself in the same way.

We, men and women, coexists equally
And no one should ever tell you differently.

You are graceful. You are gorgeous. You are radiant.
No word can describe your entire beauty.
You can achieve anything you wish
Life is at your mercy.

I have this desire that eats me alive
Where I would be, every day by your side.

Yet, as an ally and a friend
My intentions are different.
I wish to raise a proud woman
One who is independent.
Even though this is hard for me
This is the path towards equity.

I dream of waking up one day
Free from anxiety
Reassured you can live in a world
Free of inequality.

Let’s make these dreams reality
And fight for humanity.
I dream of a world where every man
Sees and treats woman the way he sees his daughter.
With respect, appreciation, dignity and equity
Like a real man would treat her.

Let’s make all of this together
And this, for you my daughter.

Marc-Olivier Fortin
